What separates RL from rule-based systems?
Rule-based systems apply fixed logic: "loyal customers get premium, new customers get discount." The DQN agent discovers context-sensitive strategies that maximize long-term revenue — sometimes sending no offer at all to highly loyal customers (they'll buy anyway, so why spend the budget?).

The Key Emergent Behavior
🚫
No Offer = Smart Strategy
The agent discovered that highly loyal, recently-visiting customers don't need offers. Sending one wastes budget and can signal cheapening of the brand.
📈
Context Over Rules
The same loyalty score with different recency triggers completely different decisions. Static rules miss this interaction — RL captures it naturally.
⚖️
Revenue vs. Loyalty Trade-off
The agent accepts slightly lower short-term revenue (smaller offer) to protect loyalty trajectory — maximizing 12-month LTV over 1-month transactions.
